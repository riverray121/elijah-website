About Elijah

Hi! My name is Elijah. My creative work is driven by personal interests in customization and technical work, physical builds often employing different curatorial techniques, playing with specialized lighting and materials. Creating objects for personal use and seeking functionality within material experiments. With a background in medical robotics and computer science, my work often exists at the intersection between the virtual and physical as I am constantly seeking to respond to our developing world in new and exciting ways.
